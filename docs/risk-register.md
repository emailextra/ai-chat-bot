# Risk Register – AI-Powered Chatbot for Customer Support

## Risk Scoring Method
- Probability is scored from 1 to 5, where 1 is very unlikely and 5 is very likely.
- Impact is scored from 1 to 5, where 1 is low impact and 5 is very serious impact.
- Risk score = Probability x Impact.
- Scores 1-4 are low, 5-8 are medium, 9-12 are high, and 15-25 are critical.

## Issue Management Strategy

An issue is different from a risk because it has already happened. The project manager will record each issue in an issue log with the date, owner, priority, action and status. Open issues will be reviewed during weekly meetings. High-priority issues must be escalated to the sponsor or lecturer quickly.

| Issue Step | Action |
|---|---|
| Identify | Team member reports the issue with short description and evidence. |
| Record | Issue is added to the issue log with owner and priority. |
| Analyse | Project manager checks impact on scope, time, cost and quality. |
| Resolve | Owner completes the agreed action and updates status. |
| Review | Team checks whether the fix worked and closes the issue. |
