# Knowledge Base – Approved FAQ Answers

This file contains the approved question and answer pairs used by the AI-powered chatbot. All answers have been reviewed by the customer support team before being added to the knowledge base.

---

## General Enquiries

**Q1: What is your customer support hours?**
Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. For urgent issues outside these hours, please use this chatbot and a support agent will follow up by the next business day.

**Q2: How do I contact a human support agent?**
Type "speak to agent" or "human help" at any time and the chatbot will transfer you to a live support agent. If no agent is available, your request will be logged and someone will contact you within 24 hours.

**Q3: What can this chatbot help me with?**
This chatbot can help you with order status, refunds, account issues, product information, service requests and general FAQs. For complex complaints or billing disputes, a human agent will be assigned.

## Orders

**Q4: How do I check my order status?**
To check your order status, log into your account and go to "My Orders". You will see the current status of each order. You can also provide your order number here and we will look it up for you.

**Q5: How long does delivery take?**
Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 business days. Express delivery takes 1 to 2 business days. Delivery times may vary during peak periods or public holidays.

**Q6: Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?**
Orders can be changed or cancelled within 2 hours of being placed. After that, the order may already be processed for dispatch. Please contact support immediately if you need to make a change.

**Q7: My order says delivered but I have not received it. What should I do?**
Please first check with neighbours or your building reception. If the item is still missing, contact us with your order number and we will raise an investigation with the delivery carrier within 1 business day.

**Q8: Can I track my delivery in real time?**
Yes. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link by email. You can use this link to follow your delivery in real time through the carrier's website.

## Refunds and Returns

**Q9: What is your return policy?**
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in their original packaging and accompanied by proof of purchase. Some items such as digital products are non-refundable.

**Q10: How do I start a return?**
Log into your account, go to "My Orders", select the order and click "Return Item". Follow the prompts to select a reason and print a return label. Drop the item at your nearest post office.

**Q11: How long does a refund take?**
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days. The amount will be returned to your original payment method.

**Q12: My refund has not arrived after 7 days. What should I do?**
Please contact us with your order number and return tracking number. We will check the status with our returns team and provide an update within 1 business day.

## Account Support

**Q13: How do I reset my password?**
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you do not see it.

**Q14: How do I update my email address or personal details?**
Log into your account and go to "Account Settings". You can update your name, email address, phone number and delivery address from there.

**Q15: My account has been locked. How do I unlock it?**
Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts for security. Click "Forgot Password" to reset your password and unlock your account. If the issue continues, contact support for manual assistance.

**Q16: How do I delete my account?**
To delete your account, please contact our support team directly as this action is permanent. We will confirm your identity and process the deletion within 3 business days.

## Products and Services

**Q17: How do I find out if a product is in stock?**
Product availability is shown on each product page. If an item is out of stock, you can click "Notify Me" to receive an email when it becomes available again.

**Q18: Do you offer a warranty on your products?**
Most products come with a 12-month manufacturer warranty. Extended warranties may be available for selected items. Please check the product page for warranty details or contact support for clarification.

**Q19: Can I get a product recommendation?**
Yes. Please describe what you are looking for, your budget and any specific requirements and the chatbot will suggest suitable products from our range.

**Q20: How do I leave a product review?**
After your order is delivered, you will receive an email invitation to leave a review. You can also log into your account, go to "My Orders" and click "Write a Review" next to the delivered item.
